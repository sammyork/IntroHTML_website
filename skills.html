How to Make Cookies

Getting Started

Ingredients Quantity
butter 1 cup (2 sticks)
grnaulated white sugar 1 cup
brown sugar 1 cup
eggs 2
vanilla extract 2 tsp
all purpose flour 3 cups
baking soda 1 tsp
baking powder 1/2 tsp
chocolate chips 2 cups

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 375℉
  2. cream(mix) butter and sugar
  3. mix in eggs and vanilla extract
  4. add the rest of the dry ingredients and mix until just combined
  5. add in chocolate chips and mix well
  6. line baking pan with parchment paper and scoop batter into sizes to your liking
  7. bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown

After letting the cookies cool, enjoy!
